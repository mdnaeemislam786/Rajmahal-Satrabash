About Rajmohol Sotrabash

Your home away from home - providing comfortable and secure accommodation for students since 2008

Our Story

Established in 2008, Rajmohol Sotrabash has been serving students from various educational institutions with quality accommodation at affordable prices. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a safe, comfortable, and conducive living environment for students pursuing their dreams.

Over the years, we have grown from a small hostel with just 5 rooms to a well-established accommodation facility with 18 rooms, housing over 200 students annually. Our commitment to quality service and student welfare has made us one of the most preferred hostels in the area.

"Our mission is to provide not just a place to stay, but a home where students can thrive academically and socially in a supportive community."

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety & Security

We prioritize the safety and security of our students with 24/7 surveillance, secure access systems, and a dedicated warden.

Community

We foster a supportive community where students from diverse backgrounds can connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Academic Focus

We maintain a conducive environment for studying with dedicated study areas, high-speed internet, and quiet hours.
